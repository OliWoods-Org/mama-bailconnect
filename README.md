## The Problem

In the US, 470,000 people are held in local jails awaiting trial — not convicted, just too poor to post bail. The median bail amount is $10,000, but even $500 can be impossible for a single mother or minimum wage worker. They lose jobs, housing, and custody while waiting.

## The Solution

An AI phone system that crowdfunds bail from your network in minutes.

1. **Call BailConnect** — AI collects your name, facility, bail amount, and up to 10 emergency contacts
2. **We alert everyone** — simultaneous SMS + voice call + WhatsApp to all contacts
3. **They contribute** — any amount via Stripe, progress tracked in real-time
4. **You come home** — auto-notification when bail is fully funded

Platform takes **ZERO fees**. Only Stripe processing (2.9% + $0.30). Built with Twilio + Stripe + Claude.

## Safety

**MANDATORY:** If caller mentions suicide or self-harm → immediate 988 handoff. Cannot be disabled.
